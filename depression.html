Depression

We all feel fed up, miserable or sad at times. These feelings don't usually last longer than a week, and they don't interfere too much with our lives. Sometimes there's a reason, sometimes not. We usually cope. However, in Depression: Your feelings don't lift after a few days, they carry on for weeks or more and even interfere with your life.
